Hi! My name is Dylan Bollard, I'm a graduate assistant working at Portland State University, and have recently joined Independent Actuaries, Inc. as an Actuarial Analyst working on pension plans. My research interests include econometrics, the economics of energy, and environmental economics. More specifically, nonparametric methods in econometrics and stochastic differential equations are budding fascinations for me, whereas my study of energy and environmental issues has been ongoing.

I earned my first Bachelor's degree in Quantitative Economics last Summer, with expected completion of my second Bachelor's degree in Mathematics this Fall, and expected completion of my Master's degree this Fall, from Portland State University. In the past, I've worked at Portland Community College as an academic tutor and teaching assistant. Before my reentry into school, I helped manage a small grocer in Portland while working on developing a film career.
